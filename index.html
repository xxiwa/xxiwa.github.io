 About me 

I am an MPhil student at the CSE of HKUST, fortunately advised by Dimitris Papadopoulos. I also work with Ioannis Demertzis. Prior to this, I completed my B.E. in Software Engineering at Xidian Univeristy. I also had a wonderful internship at Apple.

I am looking for PhD position of 25Fall! I am interested in security, systems, and applied cryptography. After spending a long time exploring wide fields, I want to settle down and conduct in-depth research in one specific direction during my PhD. My previous research involved: TEE, database query, oblivious algorithm, HE, distributed systems, edge computing, ML, and network intrusion detection.
